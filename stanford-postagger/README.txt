indonesianTweetStanfordModel is a model trained using stanford pos-tagger.

Toutanova, K., Klein, D., Manning, C. D., dan Singer, Y. (2003). Feature-rich part-of-speech tagging with a cyclic dependency network. In Proceedings of HLT-NAACL 2003, pages 252–259.

Features used are: left3words, left5words, bidirectional, bidirectional5words, generic, and sighan2005.


Training data comes from research of:

Canggadibrata, H. F. dan Bressan, S. (2012). Part of speech tagging for microblogging posts in indonesian. In The 6th International Workshop on Malay and Indonesian Language Engineering 2012, MALINDO 2012, pages 14–28.
